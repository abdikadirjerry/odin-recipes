Margherita Pizza

Description

A simple and delicious Italian pizza topped with tomato sauce, fresh mozzarella cheese, and basil leaves. Crispy on the outside and soft on the inside.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough
  • ½ cup tomato sauce
  • 200 g fresh mozzarella
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough.
  3. Spread the tomato sauce evenly.
  4. Add sliced mozzarella cheese.
  5. Bake for 12–15 minutes.
  6. Top with fresh basil.
  7. Drizzle with olive oil and serve.
