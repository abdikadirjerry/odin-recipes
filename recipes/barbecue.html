Barbecue

Description

This smoky and juicy barbecue chicken is grilled to perfection and coated in a sweet and tangy barbecue sauce. It's perfect for family dinners, weekend cookouts, or summer gatherings.

ingredients

  • 4 chicken thighs or chicken breasts
  • 1 cup barbecue sauce
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the grill to medium heat.
  2. Mix the olive oil with the seasonings.
  3. Mix the olive oil with the seasonings.
  4. Grill for 6–8 minutes on each side.
  5. Brush with barbecue sauce during the last few minutes.
  6. Cook until the chicken reaches 165°F (74°C).
  7. Let it rest for 5 minutes before serving.
