Classic Cheeseburger

Description

A classic cheeseburger made with a juicy beef patty, melted cheese, fresh vegetables, and a toasted bun. Perfect for lunch or dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1 hamburger bun
  • 1 beef patty
  • 1 slice cheddar cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato slices
  • Onion slices
  • Pickles
  • Ketchup and mustard

Steps

  1. Cook the beef patty until browned.
  2. Place the cheese on top and let it melt.
  3. Toast the burger bun.
  4. Add lettuce, tomato, onion, and pickles.
  5. Place the patty on the bun.
  6. Add ketchup and mustard.
  7. Serve immediately.
