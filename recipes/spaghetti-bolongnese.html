Spaghetti Bolognese

Description

A rich and flavorful Italian pasta dish made with ground beef, tomatoes, herbs, and spaghetti. It's comforting, filling, and easy to prepare.

Ingredients

  • 250 g spaghetti
  • 300 g ground beef
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • Salt and pepper
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions.
  2. Cook the onion and garlic until soft.
  3. Add the ground beef and cook until browned.
  4. Stir in the tomato sauce.
  5. Simmer for 15 minutes.
  6. Serve the sauce over the spaghetti.
  7. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
